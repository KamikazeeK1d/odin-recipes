Grilled Cheese

Description

Great for a snack or added to a meal. This grilled cheese sandwich tastes great and can be modified to fit your tastes. Whether you like tasty cheese or another type of cheese it will be great in this sandwich.

Ingredients
  • Slices of Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese of your choice
Steps
  1. Heat a nonstick pan at medium heat. Place butter on one side of the bread.
  2. Place the bread with the butter side on the bottom on the pan.
  3. Place slice/s of cheese on the bread in the pan.
  4. Butter one side of another slice of bread.
  5. Place other slice of bread on top of the other slice with the cheese.
  6. Once the first slice is lightly brown, flip the sandwich over in the pan.
  7. Repeat the previous steps for as many sandwiches as you would like.
